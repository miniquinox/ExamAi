What We Offer

Our platform consists of three integrated portals designed to transform how exams are created, taken, and graded:

  • Professor App: Create and set up exams with custom grading rubrics.
  • Student App: A secure environment for students to take exams.
  • Grading Portal: Access and analyze exam results efficiently.

How It Works

  1. Create an Exam: Use the Professor App to write exams with specific grading rubrics, set a grading password, and receive a unique exam ID.
  2. Exam Taking: Students use the unique exam ID to access and take the exam through the Student App, which ensures integrity through proctoring.
  3. Grading: Post-exam, enter your exam ID and grading password in the Professor Grading Portal to initiate AI grading.

    Click "AI Grade" to start the automated grading process, which completes in about 5 minutes.

  4. Review Results: View detailed grades per student and question. Students receive their grades via email and can also view class-wide statistics in the Main Grading Portal.

Features of the Grading Portal

The Grading Portal offers comprehensive analytics to assess exam performance:

  • Top 5 students by grade
  • Average class grade (visualized with a donut chart)
  • Questions that were most challenging for students
  • Detailed score distributions across the class
  • Insights into time spent on the exam and grade distributions
  • Interactive 3D Roboto spline following the mouse cursor for enhanced visualization
